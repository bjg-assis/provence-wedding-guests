Travel to and from venue

Getting to Provence

  • By air: Marseille (MRS), Lyon (LYS), or Nice (NCE), then transfer/car hire.
  • By train: High-speed rail to nearby hubs (then taxi/drive).
  • By car: Driving is often the easiest option for rural Provence logistics.

Leaving the venue

  • Most departures are expected Sunday after breakfast/brunch.
  • If booking return transfers, leave extra buffer time for rural roads.
  • Coordinate shared taxis/transfers early if not driving.
