Travel and accommodation

Getting to Provence

  • The chateau is ~2 hours by car from both Lyon and Marseille airports.
  • Train option: Montélimar station, then ~25 minutes by car to the chateau.
  • Maps: Open Google Maps

Getting around the area

  • Hiring a car is recommended if you want to explore the region.
  • The chateau reception can suggest taxi or shuttle options.
  • For private transfers: Transfeero

Staying at the chateau

  • Château Les Oliviers de Salettes is a 5-star hotel in Drôme Provençale.
  • Surrounded by lavender fields and vineyards.
  • We have reserved the whole chateau for the wedding weekend.

Venue site: chateaulesoliviersdesalettes.com
